# Share instrument-level Chart Drawings with Free Replay

## What to build

Show and save instrument-level Chart Drawings in Free Replay using the same drawing language as Trade Review. Free Replay does not show Trade entry or exit markers. Drawings created during Free Replay belong to the selected Instrument and can exist without an associated Trade.

## Acceptance criteria

- [ ] Free Replay shows Chart Drawings for the selected Instrument.
- [ ] Free Replay does not show Trade entry or exit markers.
- [ ] The reviewer can create, select, move, and delete Chart Drawings in Free Replay.
- [ ] Chart Drawings created in Free Replay are visible later in Trade Review for the same Instrument.
- [ ] Chart Drawings created in Free Replay can be saved without a Trade ID.
- [ ] Existing Trade Review drawings remain compatible with saved Trade IDs.
- [ ] Tests cover saving and listing drawings with and without Trade IDs.
